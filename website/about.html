demons

The idea of 'demons' is to explore the concept of forgetfulness caused by mental illnesses and diseases. The focus is on Dementia, specifically Alzheimer’s disease, and Amnesia. The 'player' explores game-worlds, and experience things that a person with these diseases may experience on a daily basis.

Each level is crafted so that the visitor experiences Dementia as it slowly progresses, degrading the person's quality of living. See if you can understand the meanings behind each level and how it is relevant to the people with Dementia.

Demons is a project created by Wayne Tsai as part of the COMP1720 course taught by Tim Brooks at the Australian National University, Canberra.
